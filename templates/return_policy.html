Return & Exchange Policy

At PRAUXE, we want you to love what you wear. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

🔁 Return Eligibility

  • Initiate a return within 7 days of receiving your order
  • Item must be unused, unwashed, and undamaged
  • Tags, labels, and packaging must be intact
  • Final sale, customized, and intimate items are not eligible

🚫 Non-Returnable Items

  • Innerwear, socks, swimwear
  • Accessories (earrings, etc.) for hygiene reasons
  • Clearance/Final Sale items
  • Customized or personalized products

🔄 Exchange Policy

We allow size exchanges only, subject to availability. If the size is unavailable, you may choose a refund or store credit.

💸 Refund Process

  • Refund is issued to the original payment method or as store credit
  • It may take 5–10 business days to reflect
  • For COD orders, we refund via UPI/bank/store credit

📦 How to Initiate a Return

  1. Login to your account at prauxe.com
  2. Go to “My Orders” → Click “Return/Exchange”
  3. Select item, reason, and refund mode
  4. We’ll arrange a pickup or guide you to self-ship

If self-shipping, send to:

🚚 Return Shipping Cost

  • First return/exchange is free (India only)
  • Additional returns may incur a charge
  • International returns are customer-paid

❗ Important Notes

  • Used/damaged items will be rejected and sent back
  • PRAUXE reserves the right to deny policy abuse
  • Refunds are processed only after quality check
