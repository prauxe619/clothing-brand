Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, orders, shipping, and more.

Orders & Payments

How do I place an order?

Browse our website, select the desired product, choose your size and quantity, and click "Add to Bag." Once you're ready, proceed to the checkout page, fill in your details, and complete the payment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay), Net Banking, UPI, and various digital wallets through our secure payment gateway, Razorpay. Cash on Delivery (COD) may be available for select pincodes.

Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it?

Orders can only be canceled or modified within a very short window after placement, typically before they are dispatched. Please contact our customer service immediately if you need to make changes. Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be canceled or modified.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery typically takes 3-7 business days, depending on your location. Remote areas might take longer. You can check estimated delivery times for your pincode on each product page.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email and SMS with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also find your tracking information in your account's "Order History" section.

Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?

Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery for most pincodes. You can check if COD is available for your location on the product page or at checkout using our pincode checker.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer an easy 14-day return and exchange policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, with all original tags and packaging intact. Please refer to our Return Policy page for full details.

How do I initiate a return or exchange?

You can initiate a return or exchange request through your account's "Order History" section or by contacting our customer service team with your order details.

When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after the returned item passes our quality check. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.

Products & Sizing

How do I choose the correct size?

We provide a detailed size chart on each product page. We recommend comparing your body measurements with our size chart for the best fit. If you're still unsure, feel free to contact our customer service for assistance.

What materials are your clothes made from?

Our products are made from a variety of high-quality fabrics. Specific material compositions are listed on each product's detail page under the "Product Details" section.

How do I care for my PRAUXE clothing?

Care instructions are provided on the garment's label. Generally, we recommend following the washing and drying instructions to maintain the quality and longevity of your clothing.

Account & Security

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" link in the top navigation bar. You can register using your email address, or conveniently sign up with your Google account for a quick and easy process.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

On the login page, click the "Forgot Password?" link. Enter your registered email address, and we will send you instructions to reset your password.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. We use various administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
